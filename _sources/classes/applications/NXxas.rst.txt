=====
NXxas
=====

**Status**:

  application definition, extends :ref:`NXobject`

**Description**:

  This is an application definition for raw data from an X-ray absorption spectroscopy experiment.

  This is essentially a scan on energy versus incoming/ 
  absorbed beam.
